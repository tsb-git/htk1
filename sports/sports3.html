Calcutta Sports Journalist Club Program


Calcutta Sports Journalists' Club requests the pleasure of your company at its annual awards function, which was held in Swami Vivekananda Auditorium at the State Youth Centre (Moulali Yuba Kendra) .



Sunil Chhetry, Akhtar Ali and Arun Ghosh at the Calcutta Sports Journalists' Club annual awards 2018 evening at the Moulali Yuva Kendra on Thursday. Sunil was awarded the best Sports Person of 2018 while Arun Ghosh and Akhtar Ali was honoured for their Lifetime Contribution to Sports
